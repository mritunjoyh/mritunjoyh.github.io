Intro

I am a dedicated researcher with a strong background in Artificial Intelligence, Deep Learning, Statistics, and Machine Learning. My expertise lies in developing innovative solutions for complex problems in computer vision and image processing.

With a proven track record of publications in esteemed journals and conferences, I have contributed to the advancement of the field through my research. My work includes topics such as anomaly detection robot ego view camera, enhancement of hazy images and sex identification, accent, and emotion recognition from speech samples etc.

Apart from my publications, I have also submitted a book chapter on dehazing and vision enhancement, which has been accepted for publication in IET Intelligent Multimedia Processing and Computer Vision: Techniques and Applications.

I am currently involved in ongoing research projects, including a submitted article on a transmission model-based deep neural network for image dehazing under peer review in Multimedia Tools and Applications, Springer, and an article on anomalous activity detection from ego view cameras of surveillance robots accepted for presentation in the IEEE International Joint Conference on Neural Networks (IJCNN). I am also a part of two submitted Indian patent
